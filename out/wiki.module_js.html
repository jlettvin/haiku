Source:

Returns:
HTML implementing a checklist. generates a simple table row using checkBox rules: This function makes a checklist out of lines starting with '@'. will follow the translated markdown. '@ {contents}' becomes an empty checkbox. '@+{contents}' becomes a checked box. '@-{contents}' becomes a box with an x instead of check. '@1{contents}' becomes a box with a circled 1 (same for 0-9). other characters are simply used as is in the checkbox.
